# Practice Answers (Brief)

- Low coupling is preferred.
- Abstraction hides implementation.
- Encapsulation protects internal data.
- Software includes documentation and configuration.
- Business Analyst gathers requirements.
- Architect designs the system.
- QA validates quality.
- DevOps manages deployment automation.
