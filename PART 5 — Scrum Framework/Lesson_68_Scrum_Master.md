# What is a Scrum Master?

A **Scrum Master** is a servant leader who helps the Scrum Team understand and apply Scrum effectively.

The Scrum Master focuses on:

- Team effectiveness
- Scrum adoption
- Removing obstacles
- Continuous improvement

---

# Why Does the Scrum Master Exist?

Without proper facilitation:

```text
Unclear Process
       ↓
Poor Communication
       ↓
Blockers Increase
       ↓
Slow Delivery
```

The Scrum Master helps the team overcome these challenges.

---

# Scrum Master as Servant Leader

A servant leader does not command the team.

Instead, they:

- Support
- Coach
- Guide
- Remove barriers

# Core Responsibilities

# 1. Facilitate Scrum Events

The Scrum Master ensures effective execution of:

- Sprint Planning
- Daily Scrum
- Sprint Review
- Sprint Retrospective

Example:

During Sprint Planning, the Scrum Master ensures the team understands the Sprint Goal.

---

# 2. Remove Impediments

An impediment is anything blocking progress.

Examples:

- Missing access permissions
- Environment issues
- Communication problems
- External dependencies

Example:

Developers cannot deploy because cloud access is unavailable.

Scrum Master coordinates with the responsible team to resolve it.

---

# 3. Coach the Team

The Scrum Master teaches:

- Scrum principles
- Agile mindset
- Collaboration practices

Goal:

Create a self-managing team.

---

# 4. Protect the Scrum Team

The Scrum Master protects the team from:

- Unplanned interruptions
- Unnecessary scope changes
- External distractions

Example:

A stakeholder wants urgent work added during a Sprint.

The Scrum Master ensures proper discussion through the Product Owner.

---

# 5. Promote Continuous Improvement

The Scrum Master encourages improvement through:

- Retrospectives
- Process experiments
- Team discussions

---

# Daily Activities

Typical Scrum Master activities:

Morning:

- Check blockers
- Support Daily Scrum

During Sprint:

- Facilitate communication
- Help resolve issues

Sprint End:

- Organize Review
- Facilitate Retrospective

---

# Scrum Master Does NOT Do

❌ Assign tasks to developers

✔ Developers self-organize.

---

❌ Manage people like a traditional manager

✔ Scrum Master enables the team.

---

❌ Decide product priorities

✔ Product Owner owns priorities.

---

# Scrum Master vs Project Manager

| Scrum Master | Project Manager |
|---|---|
| Servant leader | Manager |
| Coaches team | Controls execution |
| Removes blockers | Manages resources |
| Agile focus | Traditional planning |

---

# Scrum Master vs Product Owner

| Scrum Master | Product Owner |
|---|---|
| Process focus | Product focus |
| Team effectiveness | Business value |
| Facilitates Scrum | Owns backlog |

# Scrum Master Metrics

A Scrum Master may observe:

- Sprint completion rate
- Team blockers
- Cycle time
- Team improvement actions

Metrics are used for improvement, not punishment.

---

# Common Mistakes

❌ Scrum Master acting as a boss.

✔ Scrum Master is a facilitator.

❌ Scrum Master managing individual tasks.

✔ Team manages its own work.

❌ Ignoring team problems.

✔ Address issues through transparency.

# Summary

The Scrum Master enables the Scrum Team by facilitating Scrum practices, removing obstacles, coaching team members, and encouraging continuous improvement. Unlike traditional managers, Scrum Masters do not control the team. They create an environment where teams can become self-organizing, productive, and capable of delivering high-quality software.
