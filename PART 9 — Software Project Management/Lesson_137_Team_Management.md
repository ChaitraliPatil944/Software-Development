# Introduction

Software projects are built by people working together.

Even the best technology fails when teams have:

-   Poor communication
-   Unclear responsibilities
-   Conflicts
-   Lack of coordination

Team Management ensures people work effectively toward project goals.

``` text
Project Goal

      ↓

Team Formation

      ↓

Collaboration

      ↓

Successful Delivery
```

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# What is Team Management?

**Team Management** is the process of organizing, guiding, motivating,
and coordinating team members to achieve project objectives.

It focuses on:

-   Team structure
-   Communication
-   Collaboration
-   Performance
-   Conflict handling

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Why Do We Need Team Management?

Without proper team management:

``` text
Unclear Roles

      ↓

Poor Coordination

      ↓

Reduced Productivity

      ↓

Project Failure
```

Effective team management helps:

-   Improve collaboration
-   Increase productivity
-   Resolve conflicts
-   Build trust
-   Achieve project goals

# Team Development Stages

A common team development model includes:

``` text
Forming

   ↓

Storming

   ↓

Norming

   ↓

Performing

   ↓

Adjourning
```

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Forming

Team members meet and understand goals.

Characteristics:

-   Introduction phase
-   Roles are unclear
-   High dependency on leader

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Storming

Team members may disagree.

Challenges:

-   Different opinions
-   Conflicts
-   Communication issues

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Norming

Team develops:

-   Trust
-   Rules
-   Better communication

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Performing

Team works efficiently.

Characteristics:

-   High productivity
-   Strong collaboration
-   Independent problem solving

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Adjourning

Project completion stage.

Activities:

-   Final delivery
-   Documentation
-   Team closure

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Leadership Styles

## 1. Autocratic Leadership

Leader makes decisions independently.

Advantages:

-   Fast decisions

Disadvantages:

-   Less team involvement

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Democratic Leadership

Team participates in decision making.

Advantages:

-   Better collaboration

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Servant Leadership

Leader supports the team.

Common in Agile environments.

Focus:

-   Removing obstacles
-   Helping team members

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Team Management vs Resource Management

  Team Management                Resource Management
  ------------------------------ --------------------------
  Focuses on people              Focuses on all resources
  Handles collaboration          Handles allocation
  Motivation and communication   Planning and utilization

# Summary

Team Management ensures that software teams collaborate effectively and
achieve project objectives. It involves building teams, defining roles,
managing communication, resolving conflicts, and motivating members.
Strong team management creates productive teams capable of delivering
high-quality software.
