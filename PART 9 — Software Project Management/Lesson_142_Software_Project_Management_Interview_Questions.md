# Lesson 142 --- Software Project Management Interview Questions

# Part 9 --- Software Project Management

> **Objective**
>
> Prepare for Software Project Management interviews by understanding
> frequently asked questions about project planning, scope, time, cost,
> risk, resources, teams, scheduling, metrics, Agile practices, and
> real-world project scenarios.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 1 --- Project Management Fundamentals

## Q1. What is Software Project Management?

**Answer:**

Software Project Management is the process of planning, organizing,
executing, monitoring, and controlling software projects to achieve
defined objectives.

It manages:

-   Scope
-   Time
-   Cost
-   Quality
-   Resources
-   Risks

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q2. Why is Software Project Management important?

It helps:

-   Deliver projects on time
-   Control budget
-   Manage resources
-   Reduce risks
-   Maintain quality

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q3. What are the major constraints of a project?

The main constraints are:

    Scope

    Time

    Cost

    Quality

    Resources

    Risk

These are often called project constraints.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 2 --- Project Planning Questions

## Q4. What is Project Planning?

Project Planning defines:

-   Objectives
-   Tasks
-   Resources
-   Schedule
-   Budget
-   Risks

It creates a roadmap for project execution.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q5. What is Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)?

WBS divides a large project into smaller manageable tasks.

Example:

    E-Commerce Project

    ├── Requirement Analysis

    ├── Design

    ├── Development

    ├── Testing

    └── Deployment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q6. Why is WBS important?

Benefits:

-   Better organization
-   Easier estimation
-   Clear responsibilities
-   Improved tracking

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q7. What are project milestones?

Milestones are important checkpoints representing major achievements.

Examples:

-   Requirements approved
-   Prototype completed
-   Final release

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 3 --- Scope Management Questions

## Q8. What is Project Scope?

Project Scope defines:

-   Included work
-   Excluded work
-   Deliverables
-   Boundaries

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q9. What is Scope Creep?

Scope creep is uncontrolled expansion of requirements after project
start.

Example:

Initial:

    Build Website

Additional requests:

    Mobile App

    Chat System

    AI Features

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q10. How do you control scope creep?

Methods:

-   Clear scope definition
-   Change approval process
-   Impact analysis
-   Stakeholder communication

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 4 --- Time Management Questions

## Q11. What is Project Time Management?

It involves:

-   Defining activities
-   Sequencing tasks
-   Estimating duration
-   Creating schedules
-   Monitoring progress

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q12. What are task dependencies?

Dependencies define relationships between activities.

Types:

-   Finish-to-Start
-   Start-to-Start
-   Finish-to-Finish
-   Start-to-Finish

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q13. Explain three-point estimation.

It uses:

    Optimistic

    Most Likely

    Pessimistic

Formula:

    (O + 4M + P) / 6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 5 --- Cost Management Questions

## Q14. What is Cost Estimation?

Cost estimation predicts the resources and money required for project
completion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q15. Explain cost estimation techniques.

Techniques:

-   Expert Judgment
-   Analogous Estimation
-   Parametric Estimation
-   Bottom-Up Estimation
-   Three-Point Estimation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q16. Difference between cost estimation and budgeting?

  Cost Estimation   Budgeting
  ----------------- ------------------
  Predicts cost     Allocates money
  Before budget     After estimation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 6 --- Risk Management Questions

## Q17. What is Risk Management?

Risk Management identifies, analyzes, and controls possible project
uncertainties.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q18. What are types of software risks?

Types:

-   Technical Risk
-   Project Risk
-   Resource Risk
-   Business Risk
-   Security Risk

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q19. Explain risk response strategies.

Four strategies:

### Avoid

Remove the risk.

### Mitigate

Reduce impact.

### Transfer

Move responsibility.

### Accept

Accept low-impact risks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q20. Difference between risk and issue?

  Risk                 Issue
  -------------------- -----------------
  Future uncertainty   Current problem
  Preventive           Corrective

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 7 --- Resource Management Questions

## Q21. What is Resource Planning?

Resource Planning identifies and manages resources required for project
completion.

Resources:

-   People
-   Tools
-   Technology
-   Infrastructure

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q22. What is resource leveling?

Resource leveling adjusts schedules when resources are limited.

Example:

One developer cannot work on two tasks simultaneously.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q23. What is capacity planning?

Capacity planning determines whether available resources can handle
workload.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 8 --- Team Management Questions

## Q24. What is Team Management?

Team Management organizes, motivates, and coordinates team members.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q25. Explain team development stages.

Stages:

    Forming

    Storming

    Norming

    Performing

    Adjourning

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q26. How do you handle team conflicts?

Steps:

-   Understand the problem
-   Listen to both sides
-   Find common solution
-   Encourage collaboration

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 9 --- Scheduling Questions

## Q27. What is Project Scheduling?

Project Scheduling creates a timeline for completing project activities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q28. What is a Gantt Chart?

A Gantt Chart visually represents:

-   Tasks
-   Duration
-   Timeline
-   Progress

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q29. What is Critical Path Method?

CPM identifies the longest sequence of dependent tasks that determines
project duration.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 10 --- Metrics Questions

## Q30. What are Project Metrics?

Metrics are measurements used to evaluate project performance.

Examples:

-   Schedule
-   Cost
-   Quality
-   Productivity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q31. Explain Agile metrics.

Common Agile metrics:

-   Velocity
-   Burndown Chart
-   Cycle Time
-   Lead Time

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q32. What is Velocity?

Velocity measures the amount of work completed by a team in a sprint.

Example:

    Sprint 1:

    40 Story Points

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 11 --- Agile Project Management Questions

## Q33. Difference between Agile and Waterfall?

  Agile                 Waterfall
  --------------------- -----------------------
  Iterative             Sequential
  Flexible changes      Fixed requirements
  Continuous feedback   Feedback after phases

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q34. How does Agile manage projects?

Agile uses:

-   Sprints
-   Product Backlog
-   Sprint Planning
-   Reviews
-   Retrospectives

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Section 12 --- Scenario Based Questions

## Q35. Project is delayed. What will you do?

Answer:

1.  Identify cause of delay
2.  Analyze impact
3.  Adjust resources
4.  Reprioritize tasks
5.  Communicate changes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q36. Client changes requirements frequently. How will you handle?

Answer:

-   Analyze impact
-   Follow change management
-   Update scope
-   Prioritize requirements

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Q37. Team member leaves during project. What will you do?

Answer:

-   Transfer knowledge
-   Redistribute tasks
-   Adjust schedule
-   Find replacement if needed

# Summary

Software Project Management interviews evaluate whether a candidate
understands how to plan, execute, monitor, and control software
projects. Strong answers combine project concepts with practical
examples, showing the ability to manage scope, schedule, cost, risks,
resources, and teams effectively.
